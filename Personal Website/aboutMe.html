About

Hello, my name is Victoria Scholten! I'm a web developer based in Urbandale, Iowa.

When I first decided to go to DMACC, I was planning on pursuing a degree in photography. I was soon encouraged to switch over to Web Development, where I would be able to use my photography skills to enhance my designs. I have loved being able to create wireframes in Adobe XD and make my models come to life. It is the feeling you get after seeing a finished product that excites me and encourages me to continue to challenge and grow my skills in web development and design. I also love being able to add my passion for photography into the mix. I'm still growing in my abilities, but I have experience working with front-end languages such as HTML5, CSS3, Sass, and JavaScript. For back-end languages, I have worked with PHP.

My other interests outside of web development are photography, painting, ultimate frisbee, baking, reading, DIY projects, and anything outdoors.
